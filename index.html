💡 Tips for using this with AI — FAQ

Fill it out once, reuse it forever. Update your profile as your experience changes — you shouldn't have to rebuild your whole background for every application.

Stay in one conversation while you job hunt. Paste your profile into an AI chat once, then for every new job just paste the posting and ask again — the AI still has your profile from earlier in that same thread.

Why plain text? A .txt file is small and easy for any AI to read closely, so it works fine even on free plans — no need for a paid tier just to use this.

PDF or DOCX? Almost always PDF for job applications. It locks your formatting in place across devices and Word versions, while a DOCX can shift fonts or spacing depending on what opens it. That's why the prompt tells the AI to format your resume so it's ready to export as a PDF.

Filenames & versions. The prompt asks the AI to suggest a filename (like YourName_Resume_CompanyName) meant for your Downloads folder, and to version it — (2), (3), etc. — if you ask for edits later in the same chat, so nothing gets overwritten.

The bigger skill here: the more specific context you give an AI and the clearer your ask, the better anything it writes for you gets — that's worth learning beyond just this tool.
